Welcome in Data4Help!

The main scope of the Data4Help project is to provide a platform where the user can consult his/her health and location data and share these data with third-parties so that the latter can increase their business value. The application also offers two additional services: AutomatedSOS, a constant vital parameters monitoring service, thought for elderly people, to detect anomalies and, in case of emergency, call an ambulance directly to the location of the costumer, and Track4Run, a service mainly thought for runners to create and join running events and live tracking on a map participants of the runs. Both AutomatedSOS and Track4Run exploit the features offered by Data4Help.
